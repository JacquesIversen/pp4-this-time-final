FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

What types of kayaks are available for rental at Ale Lake?

We offer a diverse fleet of kayaks, including single-person recreational kayaks for solo adventures and tandem kayaks for paddling with a partner or a child.

How can I make a reservation for a kayak rental?

Making a reservation is easy! You can book your kayak rental online through our user-friendly website.

Are life jackets provided with the kayak rentals?

Yes, the safety of our customers is a top priority. Each kayak rental includes complimentary life jackets for all participants, ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience on Ale Lake.

What is the minimum age requirement for renting a kayak?

To rent a kayak, you must be at least 16 years old. However, participants aged 14-15 can enjoy kayaking with written consent from a parent or legal guardian

Are pets allowed in the kayaks?

We understand that pets are a part of the family, but for the safety and comfort of all customers, we do not allow pets in the kayaks. However, you are welcome to enjoy the scenic beauty of Ale Lake with your furry friend from the shoreline or designated pet-friendly areas.

Is prior kayaking experience required to rent a kayak from Ale Lake?

No prior kayaking experience is necessary to rent a kayak from us. Our rental service is open to all skill levels, from beginners to experienced paddlers. Before setting out on the water, our friendly staff will provide a brief orientation, covering basic paddling techniques and safety guidelines to ensure you have a smooth and enjoyable kayaking experience on Ale Lake.
