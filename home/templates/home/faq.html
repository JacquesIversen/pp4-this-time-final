FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

What safety measures are in place to protect visitors from the dangerous lake's wildlife?

Seasoning and Marinate are provided to all visitors before renting kayaks or SUP-boards.

How far should visitors stay away from the sharks and crocodiles while on kayaks or SUP-boards?

Visitors must maintain a maximum distance of 50 meters (164 feet) from the sharks and crocodiles.

Is there a designated swimming area in the lake where it's safe from the predators?

No, there is no designated swimming area due to the constant presence of sharks and crocodiles.

What is the maximum number of people allowed on a kayak or SUP-board at a time?

The maximum number of people allowed on a kayak or SUP-board is two, to ensure better stability and safety.

Are there any specific hours when visitors are not allowed to use the kayaks and SUP-boards?

Yes, when wildlife have just been feeded, this causes them to be sloppy and inactive. The SUP-boards are espicially popular after sunset

Do visitors need prior experience in kayaking or SUP-boarding to rent the equipment?

No, prior experience is not necessary as beginners serve the best taste.
